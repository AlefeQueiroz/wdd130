History

Back in 1995, amidst the lush beauty of nature and the roaring rapids of untamed rivers, Alefe Queiroz, a passionate explorer and adventurer, embarked on a journey that would leave an indelible mark on the world of outdoor enthusiasts. Fuelled by his love for white water rafting and a burning desire to share the thrill with others, Alefe took the plunge and founded White Water Rafting Co. Driven by a vision to turn his passion into a purpose, Alefe poured his heart and soul into building a company that would redefine adventure and redefine how people experienced the great outdoors. With each paddle stroke and every exhilarating trip down the rapids, Alefe's dream began to take shape, one wave at a time. But it wasn't just about the adrenaline rush or conquering the rapids; it was about the camaraderie, the shared experiences, and the sense of awe and wonder that nature bestowed upon those who dared to explore its untamed beauty. Alefe wanted to create something more than just a company; he wanted to create memories, forge connections, and leave a legacy that would endure for generations to come. Fast forward to today, and White Water Rafting Co. stands as a testament to Alefe's unwavering dedication and boundless passion. From its humble beginnings to its status as a premier adventure destination, the company continues to embody the spirit of exploration and the thrill of the unknown. With Alefe's pioneering spirit guiding its course, White Water Rafting Co. remains committed to providing unforgettable experiences, fostering a deep appreciation for nature, and inspiring adventurers to embark on their own journeys of discovery. Because for Alefe, it was never just about the rapids; it was about the journey, the people, and the stories that were waiting to be written on the waters of adventure.
